Bros

Community Guidelines

Bros is meant for small private training circles. Keep it respectful, safe, and useful.

Allowed use

Share workout and PR updates with people you know, react supportively, and keep comments focused on training progress.

Not allowed

Do not post harassment, threats, hate, sexual content, spam, scams, private information, unsafe training instructions, impersonation, or content that targets or harms another person.

Reports and blocks

You can report members or feed posts and block members locally. Reported content can be reviewed through support when needed to protect users or the service.

Moderation

Access to Bros or social features may be limited when required for safety, abuse prevention, privacy, legal compliance, or service integrity.

Contact

Send moderation concerns to support@wegojim.app.
