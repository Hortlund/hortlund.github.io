Privacy Policy

This policy explains what We Go Jim stores, syncs, and lets you delete. Last updated May 3, 2026.

Data you create in the app

We Go Jim stores workout sessions, active-workout drafts, templates, folders, exercise choices, custom exercises, notes, rest timers, goals, profile settings, avatar choices, dashboard widgets, and app preferences needed to run the workout tracker.

iCloud and CloudKit

Core features work locally on device. When iCloud is available and configured, supported profile, workout, template, and Bros data may sync through Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services. If iCloud is unavailable, the app can fall back to local-only mode.

Bros social data

If you use Bros, the app may store circle membership, invite status, workout feed posts, PR posts, reactions, reports, block-list data, display names, avatar data, and moderation context needed to operate the private social feature.

Photos, notifications, and purchases

PhotosUI may be used when you choose an avatar image. Notifications may be used for workout or app reminders if you grant permission. We Go Jim Pro purchases and subscription management are handled through App Store purchase systems and related providers used by the app.

Support and moderation

If you email support, the message, email address, diagnostic details you include, and moderation context you provide are used to respond to the request, investigate bugs, handle privacy questions, and review reports.

Retention and deletion

Data remains until you delete it, remove the app, use in-app deletion controls, or request support follow-up. Some data may remain where deletion is not technically possible, where another user controls the record, or where Apple/payment providers retain information for account, refund, tax, security, or legal reasons.

Your choices

You can use local-only functionality when cloud services are unavailable, manage notification permission in iOS Settings, block Bros members locally, report content, and use Delete My Data in the app. See Data Deletion for deletion instructions.

Contact

Questions about privacy, deletion, or moderation can be sent to support@wegojim.app.
