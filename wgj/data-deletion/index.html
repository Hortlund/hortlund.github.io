Privacy Choices

Delete My Data

We Go Jim includes in-app deletion controls and a support fallback for privacy follow-up.

Use the in-app control

Open We Go Jim, go to Settings, then Legal & Support, then Delete My Data. Confirming deletion removes local app data and requests deletion of your own synced Bros records when iCloud is reachable.

What this deletes

Your local profile, avatar, workouts, active-workout draft, templates, widgets, custom exercises, block list, pending social outbox items, cached exercise images, and your own Bros membership/reaction/workout/PR records where the app can remove them.

What may remain

The bundled exercise catalog, other members' data, Apple or payment-provider records, copies outside We Go Jim, and information retained for security, tax, refund, legal, or technical reasons may remain.

Email fallback

If you cannot access the app or need deletion follow-up, email support@wegojim.app with the subject “We Go Jim Data Deletion”. Include enough context to identify the relevant support conversation or Bros data.

Timing

Local deletion happens in the app. Cloud deletion depends on iCloud, network availability, and Apple CloudKit service state.
