Support for We Go Jim

Best-effort support for app issues, privacy questions, purchases, iCloud sync, and Bros moderation.

Email support@wegojim.app Privacy Policy Terms

What to include

Send the app version, device model, iOS version, whether iCloud is enabled, and clear steps to reproduce the issue.

Purchases

For We Go Jim Pro, use the app's restore/manage purchase tools first. Send support the visible error if restore fails.

Moderation

Report Bros members, feed posts, harassment, unsafe content, spam, or privacy concerns through support.

Troubleshooting

  • If iCloud sync or Bros is unavailable, confirm the device is signed into iCloud and has network access.
  • If workouts or templates look stale, restart the app and let sync finish before editing the same item on another device.
  • If notifications do not arrive, check iOS notification permission, Focus modes, and background settings.
  • If a purchase does not unlock, use Restore Purchases in the app and then contact support with the result.
  • For privacy or deletion follow-up, include the email address used to contact support and the relevant Bros context if any.
