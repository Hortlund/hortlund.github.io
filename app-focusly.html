About

About Focusly

Focusly is designed for people who want a lighter way to begin focused work and understand their progress over time. Sessions can be tuned around real tasks, while summaries keep the feedback clear and low-pressure.

Features

Key Features

  • Custom focus timers for deep work, short sprints, and recovery breaks.
  • Daily goals that help set a realistic target before work begins.
  • Session notes for capturing what moved forward during each block.
  • Weekly analytics with total focus time, streaks, and best working windows.
  • Gentle reminders that help restart a routine without noisy interruption.
  • Clean iOS-first interface built for quick starts and low friction.

FAQ

Is Focusly available now? Focusly is currently represented as a portfolio app page and App Store availability will be updated here when ready.

Can I customize session lengths? Yes. Focusly is planned around flexible work blocks, break lengths, and reusable focus presets.

Does Focusly require an account? The intended default is a local-first experience with account requirements added only where needed.

Contact Support

For Focusly support, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened. Support routing will connect through the main contact page as the product moves toward release.

Contact Support

Troubleshooting

If notifications do not appear, confirm Focusly has notification permission in iOS Settings and that Focus modes are not silencing alerts. If session totals look unexpected, restart the app and review any active timer before beginning a new session.

Privacy Summary

Focusly is intended to collect only the information needed to run timers, save goals, and summarize focus activity. Sensitive personal content should stay under the user's control, with clear disclosure before any optional sharing or analytics feature is introduced.

Terms Summary

Use Focusly responsibly and do not attempt to disrupt the service, copy protected app materials, or misuse support channels. Final App Store terms will be linked from this page when the app is published.

Data Handling Summary

Session history, goals, and analytics summaries are planned as app data used to power the Focusly experience. Any future cloud sync or export feature should make storage location, retention, and deletion controls clear before use.
