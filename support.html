FAQ

General questions

Where can I download the apps?

We Go Jim has a dedicated app page and public support/legal URLs. Other listed apps are portfolio concepts for now.

How do I report a bug?

Email support with the app name, device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Send a concise note describing the problem, the workflow, and what outcome would make the app more useful.

Troubleshooting

Start here first

  • Restart the app and try the action again before sending a report.
  • Confirm your device is running the latest available iOS version.
  • Check notification, Focus mode, and background refresh settings for timer-related issues.
  • Include screenshots only when they help explain the issue and do not contain sensitive information.
